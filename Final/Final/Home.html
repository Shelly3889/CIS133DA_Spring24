Aboout this store

Isla's PLants has a large, wholly outdoor plant nursery onsite. All outdoot plants are best suited to Southern Californias summer-dry micro-climate. We have a wide variety of climate-appropriate plants ranging in size from 2” to 15 gallon. Our selection includes Agaves, Aloes, Sedums, Aeoniums, Echeverias, Cacti and many more. ALthough we offer a wide arrange of outoor plants we specialize in the best indoor plants. The plants we source are climate-resilient; able to withstand the drastic changes in water availability, the harsh sun, and the cooler temperatures in the winter.
